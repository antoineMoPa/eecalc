How to use this?

Type math (example: 13*42*(3+32)/12)
Data is saved when you press GO/Enter
Copy/Paste URL to share sheet
Backspace to delete an empty cell
Use semicolon (;) to write what you would write on many cells:
a = 1; b=2; sin(a+b)

Navigation: Tab/Shift+tab, arrows

Numbers

You can use the electrical engineering notation with numbers (n,u,m,k/K,meg/M,G)
ex: 1K = 1000

Constants

pi, e (euler), i (sqrt(-1)), etc. mathjs constants

Math

sin(3.14)
sin(360deg)

b = 3 * a
b (to show the value of 'b')

Conversions

3 (m) to inches
3 in to cm
1 gal to L
1 L to cm^3
4 bytes in bits

(Note: 3 (m) = 3 meters and 3 m = 0.003)
